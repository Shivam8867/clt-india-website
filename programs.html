What We Do

Pedagogy First. Scale Enabled.

We create low-cost digital ecosystems to deliver master class content directly into rural schools, tackling the core challenge of teacher shortages in STEM subjects.

Flagship Platform

e-Patashale

Under the e-Patashale umbrella, CLT India design-builds interactive pedagogy for Science, Mathematics, Technology, and English. The programs are developed to align with local syllabus models, ensuring master lessons can be co-taught by rural class facilitators.

Key Attributes:

  • Low-cost technology delivery models built for solar and grid environments.
  • Interactive animation content, local language guides, and experiments.
  • Integrated dashboards monitoring classroom usage statistics recursively.
Teacher Co-pilot

JIGI & JIGI-JIGI App

Designed to put master teaching aids directly into the hands of local school teachers. The JIGI app bypasses hardware bottlenecks by letting teachers stream digital assets, lessons plans, experiments, and exercises on simple smartphones, casting them to shared monitors.

Platform Stats:

  • Over 36,000 active app users across rural blocks.
  • Instant offline sync capability to support areas with weak signals.
  • Includes structured assessment parameters to track student understanding.
Foundational Skills

Early Grade Reading (EGR)

To succeed in STEM, students need strong foundational reading skills. CLT India's Early Grade Reading models utilize phonic visual systems, interactive story cards, and play-based tools to build early reading fluency in local languages and English.

Impact Scope:

  • 200+ dedicated reading modules implemented in early centers.
  • Significant reduction in grade-level reading lags for rural children.
  • Designed in alignment with local primary schooling boards.
