Teacher Connect Program

Empowering educators with modern pedagogy, digital skills, and resources to transform rural classrooms across India.

Overview

Putting Teachers at the Centre of Learning

CLT India recognises that teachers are the cornerstone of quality education. True to its strategy of keeping teachers at the centre of its initiatives, CLT has successfully trained thousands of teachers in government schools where the e-Patashale program is implemented.

The Teacher Connect Program equips educators with 21st century digital skills, innovative teaching methodologies, and continuous professional development — enabling them to deliver impactful STEM education in their classrooms.

Digital Skills Training — Upskilling teachers with modern tools and technology to enhance classroom instruction.

Master Teacher Model — Expert educators design innovative STEM pedagogy that local practitioners can easily adopt.

Continuous Support — Ongoing mentoring, resources, and assessment to ensure teachers thrive.

Our Approach

How We Empower Teachers

A structured framework designed to build teacher confidence and classroom effectiveness.

Training & Upskilling

Hands-on training sessions that equip teachers with digital tools, pedagogy techniques, and subject matter expertise to deliver engaging STEM lessons.

Curriculum & Resources

Access to 15,000+ STEM videos, 2,000+ digital workbooks, and NCERT-aligned content in regional languages — all available without internet.

Peer Community

A growing network of teachers sharing best practices, lesson plans, and experiences — creating a collaborative ecosystem for continuous improvement.

Digital Tools

Supporting Teachers with Technology

Two mobile apps designed to make teaching easier and more effective.

Jigi — Teacher App

A must-have mobile app for middle and high school teachers. Access teaching points, discussion topics, cognitive ability highlights, CCE guidelines, textbook PDF downloads, and more — all at your fingertips. Available in multiple regional languages.

Jigi Jigi — Student App

Students can explore interactive lessons, conceptual videos, and assessments at their own pace. The app reinforces classroom learning and enables practice anytime, anywhere — bridging the gap between school and home.
