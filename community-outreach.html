Our Work

Community Outreach

Extending education beyond the classroom to empower communities and transform lives through meaningful engagement.

Program 01

Scholarship for Girl Students

At Children's LoveCastles Trust (CLT), we believe that education is the key to transforming lives—especially for young girls in underserved communities. Our scholarship initiative aims to support meritorious and underprivileged girl students by providing them with financial assistance to pursue higher education. By removing economic barriers and enabling access to quality learning, we are nurturing future leaders, innovators, and changemakers. Together, let's build a brighter and more equal tomorrow—one educated girl at a time.

Program 02

The After School Program

It provides an opportunity for the CLT India team to volunteer and work with children. The program includes fun activities such as movie screenings, computer skills, Spoken English, counselling, art and craft, STEM experiments, etc. The children interact with the Amazon Alexa device and have fun and interesting interaction.

Program 03

Summer Camp

CLT organizes a vibrant Summer Camp every year for the children in the community, offering a perfect blend of fun, creativity, and learning. The annual program includes a wide range of activities such as pottery, arts & crafts, environmental awareness, movie screenings, science and math days, STEM experiments, computer skills, spoken English, and career counseling. Each year, the camp provides children with a joyful and enriching experience, helping them explore new interests, develop important skills, and build lasting friendships.

Program 04

Spoken English

Children's Lovecastle Trust (CLT) recognizes the importance of English communication skills in today's globalized world. Through its Spoken English Program, CLT empowers students—especially from underserved backgrounds—with the confidence to express themselves fluently in English. The program uses interactive audio-visual content, engaging classroom activities, and structured modules designed to build vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation. By creating a fun and immersive learning environment, CLT helps children overcome language barriers and prepares them for academic and professional success.
