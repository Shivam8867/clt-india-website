Our Legacy

Pioneering Tech-Led Rural Classrooms since 1997

From our initial volunteer-led efforts to scale digital-first solutions across 11 states, CLT India has consistently driven educational transparency and accessibility.

Vision Statement

Access to quality education for all children, ensuring that constraints of resources do not restrict their capabilities and future careers.

Mission Statement

We connect students and practitioners to endless opportunities and transformative learning experiences, building tools that support local teachers.

Core Values

Operating with total transparency, placing pedagogy first in designs, relying on digital innovations to optimize cost, and maintaining public trust.

Our Milestone Journey

Tracking three decades of scalable STEM educational interventions.

1997

The Inception

Bhagya Rangachar founds Children's LoveCastles Trust (CLT India) in Bengaluru with the mission to support government schools with pedagogy assets.

2012

Manthan Award Selection

CLT receives the prestigious Manthan Award for its innovations in STEM digital pedagogy delivery across Karnataka schools.

2013 - 2017

Millennium Alliance & Digital Awards

Receives Millennium Alliance Award (FICCI and USAID) for low-cost e-learning innovations. Later honored with the Digital India Award in 2017 for e-Education scaling.

2021

BRICS Solutions Recognition

Selected as a premier SDG alignment program at the BRICS conference for promoting quality digital education targets in remote villages.
