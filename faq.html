Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CLT India

What is CLT India?

Children's LoveCastle Trust India (CLT India) is a registered nonprofit NGO based in Bangalore, working since 1997 to transform the learning experience of children through digital STEM education, e-Patashale platform, and community outreach programs.

What is e-Patashale?

e-Patashale is CLT India's purpose-built low-cost digital STEM platform for rural classrooms. It delivers interactive pedagogy in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics in regional languages for grades 5-10.

How can I donate to CLT India?

You can donate online through our secure Donate page via credit card, debit card, UPI, or net banking. All donations are eligible for 80G tax exemption under Indian Income Tax Act.

Is my donation tax-exempt?

Yes, CLT India is registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Donors are eligible for 50% tax exemption on their donation amount. You will receive a receipt and 80G certificate via email.

How can I volunteer with CLT India?

We welcome volunteers for teaching, content creation, mentoring, and community outreach. Please visit our Volunteer page to sign up.

Where does my donation go?

Your donation directly supports our education programs: e-Patashale digital classrooms, teacher training, STEM content development, and community outreach initiatives reaching underprivileged children across India.
