The second Millennium Alliance award by FICCI and USAID supports CLT to develop Early Grade Reading Resources in regional language using Ed-Tech innovation to improve reading outcomes for children in underserved communities.

1.21 million students in Karnataka are out of school, 45% belonging to early grades, the bulk of students who drop out between grades 4-8 are those who are unable to read and comprehend what is being taught in the class.

This product and process will enable children to be continuously and consistently exposed to sounds and sights of words, language through stories, activities that can be practiced both at home and schools.
