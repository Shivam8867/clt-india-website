Student Impact

e-Patashale Impact on Students

The e-Patashale program has made a significant impact on students by enhancing engagement and reducing dropout rates across various classes. With active participation from 95% of Class 10, 94% of Class 9, 89% of Class 8, 84.9% of Class 7, 84% of Class 6, and 89% of Class 5 students, the program has successfully created an interactive and inclusive learning environment.

The low dropout rates — ranging from just 5% to 16% — show that e-Patashale is not only improving learning outcomes but also inspiring students to stay connected to education and continue their academic journey with confidence.

Teacher Impact

e-Patashale Impact on Teachers

The e-Patashale program has empowered teachers with digital tools and interactive content, enabling them to deliver engaging STEM lessons even in low-resource classrooms. With comprehensive training and ongoing support, teachers have become confident facilitators of technology-enabled learning.

By bridging the teacher gap in rural India, e-Patashale ensures that every educator can provide quality education regardless of geography or infrastructure constraints.
