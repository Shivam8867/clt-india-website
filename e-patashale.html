What is e-Patashale?

Digital STEM Education for Rural Classrooms

While education technology is making its way to high-end private schools, CLT is deeply immersed in bringing education technology to government schools. CLT e-Patashale was conceptualized to bring about a transformation in rural classrooms.

The strategy is to build a replicable and scalable model where master-teachers design innovative STEM pedagogy for local practitioners and develop low-cost technology delivery models. Technology is leveraged for replication across regional languages and scale.

The entire program architecture is built around giving access to quality education in remote areas by upskilling teachers. Content can be accessed without internet, and solar panels are provided where electricity is a challenge.

The digital STEM content is for 5th–10th grades, based on NCERT syllabus. It has concept-based videos, real-time experiments, activity-based learning and assessments.
